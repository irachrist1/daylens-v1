                   Welcome to Daylens
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════


  Install in three steps:

    1. Drag the Daylens icon onto Applications →
    2. Open your Applications folder
    3. Double-click Daylens



═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  First launch: a one-time approval
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  When you double-click Daylens for the first time,
  macOS will show:

    ┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │  "Daylens" Not Opened                   │
    │                                         │
    │  Apple could not verify "Daylens" is    │
    │  free of malware that may harm your     │
    │  Mac or compromise your privacy.        │
    │                                         │
    │         [ Move to Trash ]  [ Done ]     │
    └─────────────────────────────────────────┘

  This is the standard prompt macOS shows for any
  app not distributed through the App Store. It is
  the same prompt you see on ChatGPT's Codex app
  and many other independent Mac apps.

  To approve Daylens (takes about 15 seconds):

    1. Click Done on the prompt above

    2. Open System Settings
       (click the Apple menu → System Settings)

    3. Click "Privacy & Security" in the sidebar

    4. Scroll down to the Security section
       (near the bottom)

    5. Find the line that says:
          Daylens was blocked to protect your Mac
       and click Open Anyway

    6. Enter your Mac password or use Touch ID

  Daylens will now launch. From now on you can
  just double-click it like any other app.



═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  Prefer a single command?
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  If you're comfortable with Terminal, you can skip
  the approval step entirely:

      brew install --cask irachrist1/daylens/daylens

  Requires Homebrew (https://brew.sh). The installer
  handles the approval for you.



═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  What Daylens does
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Daylens quietly records what you work on across
  your Mac — which apps, documents, and websites
  you spend time in — and builds a private, local
  timeline you can search or ask questions about.

  Everything stays on your Mac. Nothing is sent
  to a server.

  Learn more:  github.com/irachrist1/daylens
  Help:        github.com/irachrist1/daylens/blob/main/docs/INSTALL.md
